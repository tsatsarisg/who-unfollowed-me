Who unfollowed me?

Everything runs in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Did you see your follower count drop? Find out which accounts you follow but who don’t follow you back.

How to export your Instagram data
  1. Go to your Instagram settings and tap on Accounts Centre.
  2. Tap on Your information and permissions and then tap on Download your information.
  3. Tap on Download or transfer information, check Instagram and then click Some of your information.
  4. Go to the connections section and check Followers and following.
  5. Tap on Download to device, adjust the range to All time, change the Format to JSON and click on Create files.
  6. For the files to be accessible you will have to wait a few minutes and then click Download.
  7. Large accounts may get several followers files (followers_1.json, followers_2.json, etc.). Use followers_1.json — it contains your most recent followers.
